Solution Provider First, Power Generator Second

Dunhill's mission is to work with clients to solve their operational problems whether it be CO2 emissions from oil and gas operations, trapped or uneconomic reserves, lack of power in remote locations, or the high cost of power to industrial clients in Ontario.

Our Business:

Dunhill Power Corporation is a Canadian based, private independent power producer focused on clean electricity generation. Dunhill partners with First Nations and leading Energy & Industrial companies to provide solutions for dealing with flare gas or waste energy, uneconomic or under-utilized energy sources, and reducing the high cost of power in Ontario.

In Alberta and Saskatchewan Dunhill assists major exploration and production oil and gas companies reduce their carbon footprints in a cost-effective manner by converting flare gas and waste energy into clean electricity. Dunhill converts sour, trapped and uneconomic gas reserves without the need for expensive processing in to high value electricity. In remote locations and high cost power environments like Ontario Dunhill provides low cost on-site power to industrial clients. Dunhill provides clean green hydroelectricity to Governments and companies looking to reduce their carbon footprint.
