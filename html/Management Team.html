Managment Team

Dan Fisher

President

Dan is responsible for the direction of Dunhill as well developing and maintaining strategic relationships with our partners and clients. Dan will utilize his extensive development and construction expertise in managing the development and construction activities of the Company. Dan Fisher has spent over 30 years in the general construction and energy Industries in Western Canada completing over 1200 projects including: large government, petroleum, and wind farm projects. He is also a partner in oil and gas projects with Shell, Chevron and Husky.

Craig Parsons, BSc., MBA

Executive Vice President

As EVP, Craig is responsible to ensure adequate financing is in place and project economics meet standards, as set out in the company's business plan. His responsibility include business planning and budgeting, as well as, overseeing the development of power purchase agreements and client contracts. Additionally, he will oversee the engineering and development of power projects. Craig began his career on Wall Street as a financial analyst and options trader before returning to Calgary to become CFO of a TSX list oil and gas exploration firm. Other experience include founder of a wealth management firm and President of Cheque Free Corporation, a national payment processing firm.

Most recently he acted as EVP, Finance for Genalta Power, completing numerous debt and equity transactions. He also provided economic and financial expertise to other divisions of the company.

In these roles he managed public and private financings of debt and equity, as well as several mergers and acquisitions. His public market and wealth management experience in rapidly growing businesses provide the background for his support of Dunhill Power and its stakeholders.

Robert Louie, LLB, OC, Hon. Dr. LLB

Indigenous Managing Director

Robert is responsible for developing and maintaining Dunhill's First Nation relationships as well as utilizing his entrepreneurial experience in continuing to develop Dunhill Power's strategic vision. Robert has extensive relationships, experiences and networks (especially First Nations); Chairman, First Nations Lands Advisory Board; Chairman/Director, Peace Hills Trust; former Chief, Westbank First Nations (24 years); Owner/Proprietor Indigenous World Winery and several other successful businesses.

Mark Deller, MBA CGA CPA

VP, Finance

Mark's responsibilities include Strategic planning, capital structuring and management, financial and management reporting, financing, tax strategy and management, operations management, systems management, business development. He has held CFO and or senior management positions in energy service, real estate and technology companies.

Maury Parsons,
